AI Phone Assistant for Restaurants

A 24/7 AI-powered phone assistant that answers every call, reads your menu, takes accurate pickup and delivery orders, and sends them into your existing workflow — without forcing you to replace your POS.

Built for independent restaurants and small chains that rely on phone orders and are tired of losing revenue to missed calls, long holds, or outdated phone systems.

  • Answers every call instantly with a branded greeting.
  • Handles pickup and delivery orders using clear rules you define (zones, fees, prep times, cut-offs).
  • Reads and maintains your menu so callers always hear current items and prices — you don’t need to send a menu file.
